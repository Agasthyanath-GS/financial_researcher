## 3. Major Challenges and Opportunities
### Challenges:
- **Privacy Concerns:** Google faces ongoing scrutiny regarding data privacy, especially as regulations tighten globally.
- **AI Competition:** The tech industry is witnessing increased competition in AI technologies, putting pressure on Google to innovate continuously.
- **Cybersecurity Risks:** In 2026, Google anticipates challenges related to AI-driven cyber threats, including ransomware and shadow AI, which necessitate enhanced security measures.
- **Ad Revenue Pressures:** The company is also encountering rising costs in advertising, potentially affecting its traditional revenue streams.

### Opportunities:
- **AI Advancements:** With significant investments in AI technologies, Google aims to lead in developing more sophisticated AI capabilities through products like Google Gemini.
- **Cloud Computing Growth:** The increasing demand for cloud services represents a substantial opportunity for Google to capture market share and drive future revenue.
- **Digital Marketing Innovation:** As the digital marketing landscape evolves, Google's insights from user behavior can refine advertising strategies, leading to enhanced targeting and engagement.
